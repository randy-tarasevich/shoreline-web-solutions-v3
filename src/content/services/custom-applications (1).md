## You're Spending Hours on Work a Computer Should Be Doing

Every business has them — the repetitive tasks that eat two, five, ten hours a week. Building reports by hand. Copying data between tools that don't talk to each other. Chasing invoices. Updating the same information in three different places.

You know it's inefficient. You just haven't had someone build the solution.

## What We Build

### Workflow Automation

That weekly report you spend two hours assembling? We can make it generate itself. The customer data you're manually entering into three systems? We connect them so it flows automatically. Identify the bottleneck, build the bridge.

### Internal Dashboards

Real-time visibility into the numbers that matter — revenue, leads, project status, inventory — in one place, tailored to how you actually run your business. No more digging through spreadsheets or logging into five different tools to get the picture.

### Client Portals

Give your customers a branded login where they can check project status, view invoices, upload documents, or schedule appointments. It reduces the back-and-forth emails and makes your business feel more professional.

### Data Integration

Your CRM doesn't talk to your accounting software. Your booking system doesn't sync with your calendar. Your inventory tracker lives in a spreadsheet. We build the connections so your tools work as a system instead of a collection of silos.

### Custom Business Tools

Sometimes you need something that doesn't exist yet. A quoting calculator specific to your industry. A scheduling system that handles your unique constraints. A review monitoring dashboard. If you can describe the problem, we can build the tool.

## The ROI Is Simple Math

If you're spending 5 hours a week on manual work and your time is worth $75/hour, that's $19,500 a year in labor on tasks a custom tool could handle. Most of our automation projects pay for themselves within 3 to 6 months.

Beyond the time savings, automation eliminates human error, speeds up your response times, and lets you scale without adding headcount for administrative work.

## Our Process

We start with the bottleneck. What's the one thing that eats the most time or causes the most friction in your day-to-day operations? We build around that first, get it working, and expand from there.

Every project starts small and focused. No six-month timelines, no bloated scope. We ship working software in weeks, not months, and iterate based on how you actually use it.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you figure out what to build?

We start with a conversation about your workflow — what you're doing manually, where time gets wasted, and what tools you're already using. From there we identify the highest-impact automation opportunity and scope a focused first build.

### How long does a custom application take?

Most focused automation projects are delivered in 2 to 6 weeks. Larger applications with multiple features may take longer, but we always ship a working version early so you're getting value before the full build is complete.

### How much does custom software cost?

Pricing depends on complexity. A simple automation or integration starts around $1,500 to $3,000. More complex applications with dashboards, user accounts, and multiple integrations range from $5,000 to $15,000. We scope everything upfront with no surprises.

### Can it connect to the tools I already use?

In most cases, yes. If your tool has an API — and most modern business software does — we can connect it. Common integrations include Google Workspace, QuickBooks, Stripe, Calendly, CRMs like HubSpot, and many more.

### What if my needs change after launch?

That's expected. We build with flexibility in mind and offer ongoing support to add features, adjust workflows, and scale the application as your business grows. The code is yours, so you're never locked in.
