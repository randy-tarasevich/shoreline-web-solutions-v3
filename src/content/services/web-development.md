## Your Website Is Costing You Customers

Most small business websites are slow, invisible on Google, and look like every other template on the internet. Visitors land on your site, wait 5 seconds for it to load, and leave before they ever see what you offer. Meanwhile, your competitors — some with worse service than you — are showing up above you in search results because their site was built for performance.

A custom website changes that equation entirely.

## What You Get

### A Site That Loads Instantly

Every site we build loads in under 2 seconds on mobile. That's faster than 95% of your competitors. Google rewards speed with higher rankings, and visitors reward it by actually staying on your site.

### A Site That Google Can Read

We build structured data into every page — the code that tells Google your business name, location, services, and reviews in a language it understands. This is how you get those rich, detailed search listings with star ratings and business info that stand out on the results page.

### A Site That Works on Every Device

Over 60% of local searches happen on phones. Every site we build is designed mobile-first, meaning it looks and works perfectly on the screens your customers are actually using.

### A Site That's Truly Yours

No WordPress plugins to update. No Squarespace limitations. No monthly platform fees. You own the code, the design, and the hosting. If you ever want to move it, you can — no lock-in.

## Why Custom Over WordPress or Squarespace?

Template platforms work for some businesses. But if you care about speed, search visibility, and standing out from competitors, there are real limitations you'll hit.

WordPress sites carry the weight of dozens of plugins, bloated themes, and constant security updates. The average WordPress site scores 30-50 on Google's PageSpeed test. Our sites consistently score 95 or above.

Squarespace looks polished, but you have almost no control over performance, structured data, or technical SEO. You're renting space on someone else's platform, and you're limited to what their templates allow.

Custom development means every line of code serves a purpose. Nothing extra. Nothing slowing you down.
